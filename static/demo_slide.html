CIQ Ally — Competitor Content Intelligence

Rapidly contrasts client vs. competitor Amazon listings, surfaces compliant edits, and carries them through approval, finalization, and delivery.

Goal

Deliver an Ally skill that compares a client SKU against a competitor, aligns with Amazon policy, and outputs ready-to-send recommendations + final copy.

User

CommerceIQ Customer Success & retail content managers who need fast, defensible guidance to keep listings compliant and competitive.

Problem

Manual cross-checking rules, competitor copy, and final approvals is slow, inconsistent, and error-prone—especially across markets and categories.

Approach

Blend deterministic policy validation, heuristic fallbacks, and LLM suggestions; expose CLI + Streamlit + API; add eval harness and Custom GPT actions.

Solution

End-to-end pipeline (load → preprocess → compare → recommend → approve → finalize → email) with optional eval suite and on-demand reporting.

Next Steps

Expand policy extraction coverage, add more eval cases, capture session history for GPT workflows, and layer in analytics on recommendation adoption.

Tech: FastAPI, Streamlit, Gemini, Mailjet, Render Demo: /compare → /validate → /finalize → /email → /eval
